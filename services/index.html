Core Service

Business Freight Delivery

Our flagship service. Atlas runs dedicated commercial delivery routes throughout Kingsville and Demotte, moving goods between businesses on a schedule that keeps operations running smoothly.

From supply deliveries to outbound shipments, we handle the full range of B2B freight needs with professionalism and punctuality.

  • Regular scheduled routes through both service cities
  • Pickup from your business location or warehouse dock
  • Suitable for pallets, bulk goods, and commercial packages
  • Consistent driver relationships with your team
Residential

Home Delivery

Not everything ships to a dock. Atlas Shipping delivers to homes throughout Kingsville and Demotte with the same care we give every commercial client. Your residents and customers get real delivery service, not a phone-tag nightmare.

  • Door-to-door delivery throughout our service area
  • Careful handling for personal and household items
  • Scheduled delivery windows when available
  • Same driver reliability as our commercial routes
Heavy Freight

Semi Truck Shipping

When the load is too large for a delivery truck, our semi fleet steps in. Full-size freight capability for businesses moving large quantities of goods, oversized items, or bulk commercial shipments across our service territory.

  • Full semi-trailer capacity for bulk loads
  • Commercial dock pickups and drop-offs
  • Experienced drivers trained for heavy freight
  • Ideal for manufacturing, supply chain, and retail distribution

Air Freight
