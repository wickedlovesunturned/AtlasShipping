Our Story

Built to move
this community forward

Atlas Shipping was founded in Kingsville with one goal: give local businesses and residents the kind of reliable, honest delivery service they actually deserve.

Who We Are

More than a
delivery company

Atlas Shipping is a locally owned and operated shipping company based in Main Town Kingsville. We run our own trucks, employ our own drivers, and take full responsibility for every shipment that passes through our hands.

We serve businesses first. Our routes are built around commercial clients who need freight moved on a schedule they can rely on. But we also serve the homes and families of Kingsville and Demotte, because this is our community too.

When long-distance or time-sensitive air freight is needed, we partner with Trevor Air Cargo, whose experienced pilots extend our reach beyond what any truck can do alone.

Our Mission

Why we exist, and
what drives us

Our Purpose

To be the most dependable shipping company that Kingsville and Demotte have ever had. We exist to make logistics simpler, more reliable, and more human for every business and household we serve.

Who We Serve

Primarily businesses, from retail operations to warehouses that need daily freight runs. We also serve residential customers who need reliable home delivery. If you are in Kingsville or Demotte, we are your carrier.

Our Standard

On-time delivery is not a goal, it is a baseline. We hold ourselves to a standard that says your shipment should arrive when and where it is supposed to, every single time. No excuses, no runaround.

Our Commitment

This is a long-term relationship, not a transaction. We are invested in the growth of the businesses we serve and the communities we call home. Atlas is here to stay.

What We Stand For

The principles that
guide everything we do

01

Honesty Over Everything

We do not overpromise. If there is a problem with a delivery, we tell you. No hiding, no spin. Transparency is how we build trust that actually lasts.

02

Local Accountability

We are not a national chain with a call center somewhere else. When something goes wrong, you can reach someone in Kingsville who will fix it. That is the local difference.

03

Owned Infrastructure

We own our trucks and we train our drivers. That means we control the quality of every delivery end-to-end. No third-party handoffs, no mystery about where your freight is.

04

Business Comes First

We are built for commercial clients. Our routes, our scheduling, and our service model are designed around the needs of businesses that depend on logistics to operate.

05

Care for Every Package

Whether it is a pallet of industrial equipment or a single box to someone's front door, it gets handled with the same care. The size of the shipment does not determine the effort we give.

06

Partnership Over Transaction

We want to be your long-term carrier partner, not just the company that shows up once. When you grow, we grow. Your success is directly tied to ours.

Our Fleet

The vehicles that
make it happen

Delivery Trucks

Our core residential and business delivery vehicles. Agile, dependable, and right-sized for urban and suburban routes throughout Kingsville and Demotte.

Semi Trucks

For heavy freight and bulk commercial shipments. Our semi fleet handles the large loads that keep businesses stocked, supplied, and running on schedule.

Air via Trevor Air Cargo

Extended reach through our air freight partnership. Trevor Air Cargo's pilots handle all aviation operations, giving Atlas clients access to air-speed delivery when ground routes are not fast enough.

Trevor Air Cargo - Official Atlas Air Partner

When ground shipping is not enough, Atlas Shipping works alongside Trevor Air Cargo, an airplane carrier operated by experienced cargo pilots. The partnership gives our clients the option to move freight by air when speed and distance demand it, all coordinated through Atlas.
