Our Story

Built to move this
community forward

Atlas Shipping was founded in Kingsville with one goal: give local businesses and residents the kind of reliable, honest delivery service they actually deserve.

Who We Are

More than a
delivery company

Atlas Shipping is a locally owned and operated shipping company based in Main Town Kingsville. We run our own trucks, employ our own drivers, and take full responsibility for every shipment that passes through our hands.

We serve businesses first. Our routes are built around commercial clients who need freight moved on a schedule they can rely on. But we also serve the homes and families of Kingsville and Demotte, because this is our community too.

When long-distance or time-sensitive air freight is needed, we partner with Trevor Air Cargo, whose experienced pilots extend our reach beyond what any truck can do alone.

What We Stand For

The principles that
guide everything we do

Six values that shape every hire, every route, and every delivery we make.

01

Honesty Over Everything

We do not overpromise. If there is a problem, we tell you. Transparency is how trust that actually lasts gets built.

02

Local Accountability

We are not a national chain. When something goes wrong, you reach someone in Kingsville who will fix it. That is the local difference.

03

Owned Infrastructure

We own our trucks and train our drivers. That means we control the quality of every delivery end-to-end with no third-party handoffs.

04

Business Comes First

We are built for commercial clients. Our routes, scheduling, and service model are designed around businesses that depend on logistics.

05

Care for Every Package

Whether it is a pallet of equipment or a single box to someone's front door, it gets handled with the same care every time.

06

Partnership Over Transaction

We want to be your long-term carrier partner, not just a company that shows up once. When you grow, we grow.
